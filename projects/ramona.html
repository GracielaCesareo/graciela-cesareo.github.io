E-commerce

RAMONA BAZARES

Ramona Bazares is an entrepreneur project focus in second hand clothes selling

My role: UX/UI Designer

Timeline: Feb 2020 - 1 week

  • Methods & Tools
  • Ux Research
  • Information Architecture
  • UI Design
  • Figma
  • Photoshop

overview

We wanted to help local shops to improve their online presence to be more competitive in the market. In this project we focussed our efforts on delivering great shopping experiences for end users. We helped RAMONA to create her e-commerce from scratch, higlighting their value proposal and matching with our user needs.

The current vintage clothing trend, searching for unique style combined with sustainability, has become important to sell and buy on second-hand markets, increasing the interest by new consumers.
