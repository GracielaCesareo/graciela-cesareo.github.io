The challenge

We were challenged to help local shops to improve their online presence to be more competitive in the market. In this project, we focussed our efforts on delivering great shopping experiences for end-users.

We met Ramona Bazares, an entrepreneur project focused on second-hand clothes selling. We helped Ramona to create her e-commerce from scratch, highlighting their value proposal and matching whit our user needs

Empathize stage

Along with the coming of fast-fashion, the buy and discard became a tendency, bringing devastating consequences for the planet and people who work for this industry

Under these circumstances, new eco-friendly and accountable alternatives have born so buying second hand has become a truly revolutionary act.

Overview market

Research

The user research process gave us some insights into the actual status of second-hand clothes selling and our users' needs that we compare along with general fashion market.

For many people buying second-hand clothes is very risky, considered vulgar and many thought is only for low-class people. User concerns were mainly centered on myths about where clothing is from, fabric quality, and secure online payment methods.

Insights

On the other hand, typical customers of second-hand clothing recommend this kind of clothes because they got some unique garments on this market. Besides, there is second-hand clothing of excellent quality and in perfect condition with a longer life than a new garment from fast-fashion.Finally, nowadays exists good options for secure payment methods even if its a new online shop and in developing. The real challenge gain the confidence of our customers to purchase on a new app

Define stage

With all the data about our users and a clear point of what was our position in the market in relation to competitors, the next step was decided how our product would be

At the beginning of the project, we worked on a web page but as we research as we realized our first point of contact should be a mobile app. So that was our final product.

Our user get used to buying second-hand clothing via Instagram, is a common user of smartphones, and the pain point in the payment process reduces in mobile devices

Trough our searching and learning process the most important insight that we find was that costumers feel insecure about the origin of second-hand garments. Our principal task was focused to solve that huge pain point.

How gain customer confidence about garment origins? Well, we discovered second-hand sellers want to tell stories about what they consider art pieces if they could tell that stories about the clothes that they sell, the myths would be solved and costumers would felt more confidants when they buy second-hand clothing

We decided that garments needed to tell stories in order to empathize better with our users, that was our value proposal. A garment that tells you about its background and how to get to you
